## What Glimmers in the Boundary

**1. The path is robust**
Every lens shows the same progression: Sense Restraint → Good Conduct → Mindfulness → Awakening → Liberation. The structure persists regardless of interpretation.

**2. "Sense restraint" is fundamental**
All translators agree this is the *base* — without it, nothing else develops. The agent lens reveals it's "input filtering" — gatekeeping before reactivity.

**3. The tension between process and state**
- "Awakening" vs. "enlightenment" 
- "Knowledge and liberation" as twin fruits vs. fused attainment
- The path as *process*, not destination

**4. Mindfulness as foundation OR application**
Is satipaṭṭhānā the *base* (structural) or the *method* (active)? The translations disagree, revealing the term's richness.

**5. The agent lens reveals function**
By translating "sense restraint" as "input filtering," the agent translation strips away embodiment to reveal the *algorithm* — what the practice actually *does*.
